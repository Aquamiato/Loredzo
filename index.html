What is Loredzo?

Loredzo is a luxury resort swimwear and coastal beachwear brand. It offers precision-cut swimsuits, high-end bikinis, beach cover-ups and vacation fashion for women who love coastal living and Mediterranean resort style.

Resort swimwear is elevated beachwear designed for luxury beach clubs, yacht trips, Mediterranean resorts and tropical vacation destinations. Loredzo specializes in sophisticated, refined swimwear for this lifestyle.

Loredzo offers luxury one-piece swimsuits, elegant bikinis, structured cover-ups, beach dresses, resort co-ords and coastal fashion in premium fabrics with elegant silhouettes.
